About Us

Yorkshire Craftsmanship,
Every Time.

Alltek Plastering & Rendering Ltd is a specialist plastering, rendering and drylining team working across South & West Yorkshire — built on 20+ years of hands-on trade experience by founder Ross.

From new-build housing developments and care-home contracts through to renovations, extensions, schools and one-off bespoke houses, we work to the same standard whatever the scale. Modern render systems — silicone, acrylic, monocouche — alongside MF ceilings, grid ceilings, fire and sound boarding. The proper way, finished tidy, on schedule.

★ Awarded Redrow's Seal of Excellence on the Meadow Vale development — recognition that our workmanship meets the housebuilder's toughest quality standards.
